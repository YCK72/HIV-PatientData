This dashboard presents all analyses for two planned manuscripts. Paper 1 characterises the HIV–chronic pain phenotype: demographics, pain distribution, pain locations, and adjusted logistic regression. Paper 2 examines clinical correlates: comorbidities, ART regimens, psychiatric burden, smoking, sleep, polypharmacy, and advanced multivariate analyses. The Correlations tab contains the original 12 exploratory analyses. Statistical tests: Point-Biserial r, Spearman r, Chi-Square, and Logistic Regression.
